<h1 style="text-align: center;">Towards A Self-Regulated Society of Robot Workers!</h1>
<p>As a PhD candidate in Cognitive Robotics Research Centre, University of Wales, Newport I have been working in this effort of developing a future society of robot workers. The main theme of this research is to make these robots self-regulated by their decentralized bottom-up interactions as found in the society of ants and other biological species. I have developed a manufacturing shop floor application where a society of real e-puck (mobile) robots demonstrate their self-regulated actions inspired by our generic model of division of labour.
