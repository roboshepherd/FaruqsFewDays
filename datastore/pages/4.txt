<h1 style="text-align: center;">Towards A Self-Regulated Society of Robot Workers!</h1>
<p>Welcome to Faruq's-Few-Days! Currently I am a PhD candidate at Cognitive Robotics Research Centre, University of Wales, Newport. As a part of our EPSRC project "Defying the rules - How self-organizing systems work", I have been working towards developing a future society of robot workers. The main theme of this research is to make these robots self-regulated by their decentralized bottom-up interactions as found in the society of ants and other biological species.</p>
<p>I have emulated a manufacturing shop floor scenario where a society of real e-puck mobile robots demonstrate their self-regulated actions inspired by our generic model of division of labour. As an example, follow these YouTube clips:</p>
